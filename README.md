# Smart Career Domain Selection & Portfolio Analyzer  
*A Career Guidance and Readiness Platform for Engineering Students*

---

## 📌 Overview

Engineering students often struggle with choosing the right career domain and building an effective resume or portfolio aligned with industry standards. These decisions are frequently influenced by peers, social media trends, or incomplete information, resulting in misaligned career paths and reduced employability.

This project proposes a unified web platform that assists engineering students in:
- Selecting suitable career domains based on their profile
- Evaluating resumes and portfolios using domain-specific benchmarks
- Receiving actionable improvement suggestions
- Assessing overall career readiness through a structured score

The platform is designed to be branch-aware, student-centric, and industry-aligned.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

There is currently no unified, branch-aware digital system that helps engineering students simultaneously:
1. Identify suitable career domains based on their interests, skills, academic exposure, and goals.
2. Understand why their resumes or portfolios are ineffective for those domains.

As a result, students often choose unsuitable domains, build generic portfolios, and remain unaware of their actual career readiness for internships, placements, or higher studies.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

The proposed system integrates career domain recommendation with portfolio evaluation into a single decision-support platform.

The system:
- Collects student profile data (branch, skills, interests, goals)
- Recommends suitable career domains using rule-based logic
- Evaluates resumes/portfolios against domain-specific standards
- Generates improvement suggestions and a readiness score

This approach bridges the gap between career choice and employability preparation.

## 🧩 System Modules

1. User Profiling Module  
   Captures branch, skills, interests, academic level, and career goals.

2. Domain Recommendation Engine  
   Uses rule-based logic and domain mappings to suggest suitable career paths.

3. Portfolio Analysis Module  
   Evaluates resumes and portfolios against predefined domain benchmarks.

4. Recommendation & Scoring Module  
   Generates improvement suggestions and calculates readiness scores.
